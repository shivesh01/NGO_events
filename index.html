This World Art Day, iVolunteer has planned to celebrate art on April 11th, 2021 by calling in all the different types of artists virtually to give the shelter home kids a beautiful glimpse of what art is. If you think that you know any sort of art and that you can transfer the same knowledge to kids, do register today and help us make this day a memorable one. It can be anything like singing, painting, sketching, DIY crafts, playing any instrument, etc. If you do not want to teach but just want to join as a participant and enjoy the time with kids, you are most welcome!
